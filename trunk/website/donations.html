Donations

Introduction

Please consider donating to the Libranet Basics book project. Donations will be used to pay the hosting and domain name. We adhere to the following rules concerning donations:

  • The money will only be used for webhosting and domain costs of libranet-basics.org.
  • There is an open administration for people who are interested, so everybody can see where money goes, and how it is spent. Please, subscribe to the finance list if you are interested in tracking the finances of the project.
  • Every donation is welcome, from a few bucks to larger amounts of money, and all donations will be treated equal.
  • People who donated will have their name listed on a special donation page on the website.
  • If there is more money than needed for a year it will be saved for the next year(s).

You can donate by using PayPal. Our PayPal account is donate at libranet-basics dot org. You can also use the PayPal button shown below.
